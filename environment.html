Environment

Environment is one of the focused thematic areas chosen by the company for implementing CSR projects. Under the theme, the Company has been directing its resources on solid waste management, improving air quality, tree plantation, renewable energy and watershed management.

CII Foundation

Know More

The project implemented by CII Foundation aims to promote sustainable solutions to the farm stubble burning problem. The project is being implemented in 20 villages of Karnal district, Haryana. CII Foundation under the proposed Crop Residue Management Initiative will encourage, train and support farmers to give up the conventional practice of stubble burning and use suitable tools and technologies for managing crop reside in an environment friendly manner, especially through adopting largescale in-situ recycling of crop residue.

IPCA Foundation

Know More

Indian Pollution Control Association (IPCA): The project aims to create a sustainable supply chain, strengthen the collection & segregation of plastic waste and devising a model to resolve the plastic waste management issues in Delhi & NCR areas. In Phase-1 a Plastic Recycling Facility (PRF) was set up in Greater Noida & a Material Recovery Facility (MRF) was set up in Geeta Colony, East Delhi. The MRF has a capacity to receive and segregate 60 MT of Waste per month and PRF has capacity to process 300 MT per month.

In Phase 2 more MRFs are being set up to further integrate plastic waste supply chain. Two more MRFs are being set up in East Delhi to receive and segregate plastic waste. In addition to this one more MRF in Greater Noida is sanctioned for door to door collection, segregation and recycling of waste in collaboration with Greater NOIDA Development authority.

SAAHAS Foundation

Know More

The project objective is to ensure source segregation of waste in Sikanderpur village, spread awareness about waste management, build capacities of city municipal corporation officials and sanitation staff and, enable resource recovery from waste. Infrastructure support has also been provided to establish a wet waste processing plant. The project covers 2500 households and 3000 commercial establishments.

Sankalptaru Foundation

Know More

This environment based project aims to plant 30,000 tree saplings of indigenous varieties (Neem, Peepal, Papdi etc.) in the Aravalli Hills, Sohna Block, Gurugram. The objective of the project is to sustain and add green cover at the 30 hectare plantation site. The project will results in significant amount of carbon sequestering and enriching the biodiversity. As of April 2022, 3075 trees of different plant varieties such as Jamun, Rosewood, Neem etc., have been planted.

Skill Council for Green Jobs

Know More

The project aims to design, supply, erect and commission 300 kWp Grid tied Solar PV plants at 2 government hospitals in Delhi. The project will results in power generation of approximately 4,20,000 kW annually. This will aid in reduction of CO2 emission and will render cumulative saving on electricity cost for the hospitals. As of April 2022, 120 kWp solar plant has been setup at Madan Mohan Malviya Hospital, one of the government hospitals in Delhi and it is expected to be functional in coming months.

Tarun Bharat Sangh

Know More

The project aims to create water security for 9 villages in Nuh District, Haryana. The need assessment study has shown that the underground water levels are fast depleting in Nuh, posing threat to livelihoods of farmers and villagers of the area. To tackle the challenge, 15 rainwater harvesting structures and 20 water tanks will be constructed. The project will also equip 50 farmers with sprinklers for water efficient irrigation. As part of the project, local communities have been mobilised for participatory water resources management.

Education

With an aim to improve quality of education in government schools, SBI Card has taken up projects to enhance digital infrastructure and set-up tinkering labs. It is hoped that the projects will not only expose students to new era technology but will also equip the schools to conduct classes in hybrid model. The projects involve supporting teachers at the government schools to integrate new technology and new methods in pedagogy.

Know More

Healthcare

During COVID-19, weakness of our health infrastructure was exposed like never before. This was starker in public health facilities which are accessed by majority of Indian population putting extreme pressure on already stretched system resulting into lack of access to timely, quality and affordable health services. With the intention to improve the situation, SBI Card together with its implementing agencies has invested in health infrastructure in government and charitable hospitals with an aim to strengthen these facility which are accessed by underprivileged communities. Further, the company has piloted and scaled up telemedicine services in the remote Mewat region with an aim to provide quality affordable health services at door-step and there by helping in reducing pressure on Community Health Centers, Primary Health Centers and District Hospitals.

MAMTA Health Institute for Mother and Child

Know More

SBI Card in collaboration with MAMTA Health Institute for Mother and Child, has been supporting the project with an aim to improve mental wellbeing and resilience among adolescents and young married women through community led approach in Government of Indias RMNCH+A strategy. Phase 1 of the project covered 23757 beneficiaries through behavioral change communication based sessions. Phase 2 started in February 2022 and is extending mental health awareness to the larger population of the community through focused interventions developed during the first phase.

Manzil Mystics

Know More

This project is focusing on imparting life skills and mental well-being through music among students of government and low income schools, communities and organisations working with such communities. The project intends to enable children express their hopes, feelings, ideas, happiness, anger and frustration through music. As part of the project, a mobile music studio has been developed and is being used to train students in their schools and communities. So far, 4370 children have received music exposure/workshops on continuous basis and 100 youth from low income background have been supported with training on sound engineering, film making and graphic designing.

Mental Health Foundation

Know More

COVID-19 and subsequent lockdowns have brought back focus on Mental Health. With an aim to develop resilience in students of standard 6-8 enrolled in Delhi NCR schools, audiovisual workshops based on bio-psycho and socio-cultural models of well-being have been included in the project. The workshops aims to improve adaptive skills, handle stress, increase cultural rootedness and strengthen value system which will help them to lead a disciplined and fulfilling life. The project also supports publication of e-magazine Mansik Swasthya Patrika to spread awareness on mental health in Hindi language.

RTIT

Know More

Round Table India Trust: The project aims to empower people with locomotive disorder with advance mobility devices, by providing a customized and detachable combination of wheelchair and scooter . The project involves providing 154 wheelchairs cum scooter to the identified beneficiaries across India based on parameters like medical diagnosis through clinical assessment and level of need based on economic background. The vehicles come with a three-year warranty and an insurance cover. By April 2022, 78 wheelchair cum scooter has been delivered at different locations of Chennai, Pondicherry and Madurai.

Sai Sanjeevani

Know More

Congenital heart disease (CHD) poses a significant global health and economic burdendespite advances in treating CHD reducing the mortality risk, globally CHD accounts for approximately 300,000 deaths yearly. The cost of treating CHD in private hospital varies from 2-8 lakhs making it out of reach for economically weaker sections. With an aim to support such families, Sai Sanjeevani Hospitals have been providing free of cost treatment for CHD patient. SBI Card with an aim to further strengthen the hospital infrastructure, supported supply of critical equipment at Sai Sanjeevani Hospital, Palwal to add 10 beds to its ICU facility and strengthened its Operation Theater infrastructure. Since addition of SBI Card supported equipment in February'22, 203 cases have been treated by the hospital till March'22.

SHEOWS

Know More

Saint Hardayal Educational & Orphans Welfare Society: Under this project, 277 destitute and abandoned elderly beneficiaries were rescued in Delhi/NCR and were provided with nutritious food, clothes, shelter, regular health checkups and physiotherapy sessions during FY 21-22. In addition to rescue, 200 unique beneficiaries were supported under the project , wherein nutritious meals were being served along with general health check-ups and physiotherapy sessions.

Smile Foundation SOW

Know More

Smile on Wheels' project aims to provide preventive healthcare facilities to underserved communities of Delhi, Gurugram, Chennai and Agra (Rural and Urban). In the initial phase which lasted till November 2021, around 73,758 OPD consultations were held across 5 locations resulting into increased access to preventive healthcare services in slum and rural pockets. In the ongoing phase, the project is operational in Delhi, Gurugram and Agra (Rural and Urban) along with Nuh, an aspirational district of Haryana. Approx. 25,884 OPD consultation were facilitated under the ongoing phase.

Smile Foundation Telemedicine

Know More

In 2021, SBI Card supported a Telemedicine project in Nuh, Haryana - an aspirational district. The aim of the project is to establish telemedicine facility at Health Sub-Centers with the objectives that people in remote villages can access quality basic health services at their doorstep while reducing the pressure on Primary and Community Health Centers. The project has been designed to provide basic diagnostic tests, consultation with general physician and specialist doctors, providing medicine through a vending machine maintained by the government. In first phase, one telemedicine center was established in Doha Sub-Center. Over a period of 10 months, 3319 consultations facilitated at Doha Sub-center. Learning of the phase 1, helped us making relevant changes/updates in upgrading 9 Sub - Centre's established in Phase 2.

Know More

Skills Development

India has one of the youngest population in the world. The need is to capitalize on the demography dividend of the country. However, employability of our youth remains a challenge due to lack of proper training. The situation is more challenging for people with disability and women. Hence, SBI Card has undertaken skill program for people with disability and young women.

CADRRE

Know More

Centre for Autism and Other Disabilities Rehabilitation, Research, and Education (CADRRE): This project aims to pilot a free Employability Training Program that equips young adults on the Autism spectrum and have finished secondary school education with skills required to be employable. This comprises of level 1 training of 4-6 months on life skills, communication skills and social skills training, followed by a level 2 training of 3-6 months on job specific skills and a level 3 training of 3-4 months of onsite apprenticeship with a potential employer. So far, the first batch of 8 students have completed their level 1 training. Through this project, 100 youth with Autism will be trained and their employability will be enhanced. The aim is to establish a workable and scalable skill development program for youth with Autism.

HSSC

Know More

Healthcare Sector Skill Council: The project will train 560 eligible candidates under 4 different COVID-19 Frontline Workers job roles ; to reduce the stress on the healthcare sector and strengthen the skilled human resource capacity to fight the COVID-19 pandemic in the country. The focus is on practical and hands-on skilling by leveraging industry partnerships to provide on-the-job training and placement.

NAB

Know More

National Association for the Blind: The project supported technology training, personality development and soft skills training for 100 youth with visual impairment. Further, the project also supported the education of 250 school children with visual impairment from February'21 to April'21. The project also supported the renovation of toilets, establishment of a call center (including equipment) and installation of a 65 kWp solar power plant at NAB's Dwarka center.

Noida Deaf Society

Know More

The project aims to develop professional job-related skills in deaf youth through focused skilling activities and enabling them to be self reliant members of society. So far, 623 deaf youth have got training, out of which 247 candidates got job placement. Corporates are being sensitise to develop inclusive ecosystem to utilise the strength of deaf youth. So far, 45 corporates have been sensitised regarding person with disability hiring and employment for the trained youth.

Sarthak Educational Trust

Know More

The project aims at enabling 500 youth with disability to live financially independent lives through Sarthak SBI Card JEET (Job Entrepreneurship and Empowerment Training) skilling and placement support program at its Kolkata and Ahmedabad training centers. The program encompasses basic skill building, sector specific training, pre-employment training, allied activities, regular assessments and feedback.

Know More

The 'Future Perfect' initiative for skill development and entrepreneurship project was aimed at improving the employability of India's underprivileged youth. The project focused on helping them acquire English language skills and other new age employability enhancing skills, followed by job placements in service sector and lifelong support.

Disaster Management

COVID-19 pandemic impacted every aspect of our modern life resulting into unprecedented loss of life and economic meltdown. With an aim to help the most marginalized sections of the society, the Company has taken up various relief and response projects.

Akshaypatra

Know More

As a part of COVID-19 disaster relief, 18,000 happiness boxes were distributed to students enrolled in government schools of Ranga Reddy district, Telangana. Every kit had ration and supplies like pulses, chana, salt, sugar, atta, cooking oil, biscuits, note books, tooth paste and brush, soap, stationery items, sanitary napkins for girls etc.

Doctors For You

Know More

This project was designed to provide home care support to asymptomatic and mild symptomatic patients of COVID-19 across Gurugram. The aim was to reduce rush to hospital and thereby free up hospitals for treatment of severe and complex cases of COVID-19 infection. Counselling through Teleconsultation were provided along with home visit to infected patients, where they were provided with medical kits and supplies including medicines, oximeter, thermal scanner/digital thermometer, N95 mask, sanitizer etc. as per doctors prescription. Progress of patients were regularly monitored through follow-up calls.

Healing Field Foundation

Know More

The project involved supporting healthcare equipment and medicine for treatment of COVID-19 Patients at Telangana Institute of Medical Sciences through Healing Field Foundation. Under this project, drugs & equipment support was provided to bridge the gap in the supply and thereby support healthcare professional in providing best possible treatment and care to COVID-19 patients. As part of the project ICU Fogging machine, High Flow Nasal Cannula Machines, Cold Storage for Dead Bodies, Ice Lined refrigerators, etc. were supported for timely treatment.

I am Gurgaon

Know More

In order to respond to excessive load on medical infrastructure during COVID-19 pandemic including transportation of patients to healthcare facilities, 2 ambulances equipped with advanced life saving equipment including ventilators was provided to Gurugram administration.

IAHV-RNT Udaipur

Know More

International Association For Human Values (IAHV) RNT Udaipur: RNT Medical College, Udaipur is a major public healthcare institution catering to patients from adjoining districts of South Rajasthan. In 2021, to strengthen the infrastructure to support critical cases of COVID-19, 14 ICU ventilators were supported by SBI Card. This helped to establish RNT Medical College as a major treatment center for COVID-19 in South Rajasthan.

Plan International

Know More

One Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) tank of 10,000 liter capacity was also established at Rajiv Gandhi Super Specialty Hospitality.
